Chief Usher

Sophia Beckett

Sophia's natural talent took her far and wide. When they met Phil they were working on a piece for Andrew Lloyd Webber. Phil and Sophia became firm friends, and started working on small projects together. This is where the trouble started as, naturally, the high level of contact between Sophia and Phil caused Sophia to become quite ill. Sophia begged Lloyd Webber to give them time off, however Andrew Lloyd Webber turned ugly.

Ever since Sophia has been happily working for The Classic Theatre, despite the debilitation that working with such company causes. Sophia is an excellent example of the tenacity that is necessary for all of our employees.
