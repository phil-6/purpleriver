Owner

Phil Reynolds

Phil claims to have been with the company since its very beginnings way back in 1016. No one believes this. None of us here at the Classic Theatre quite know how he came to own the place but he certainly has been here for as long as any of us can remember. It is possible that he has owned the Classic Theatre for all of its 996 years, but that would just be rather creepy.

Phil does well as the owner leaving most of the running of the theatre to his carefully selected directors. Most of the staff have their story of how he cherry picked them from their old jobs and enticed them to work for the Classic Theatre.
