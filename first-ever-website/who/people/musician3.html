Musician

Jeff Edwards

Jeff Horatio Winston Edwards was born in Edinburgh to strict Roman Catholic parents, who dreamed their son would one day joining the clergy. However one fatefully cold December evening whilst bringing soup and blankets to the local homeless, Jeff met an old drunk busker who asked Jeff to hold his guitar while he ate, the man died eating that bowl of minestrone.Jeff was left holding that guitar, which he took as a sign that he was destined to be a musician. Jeff has extensive experience in the industry, being part of the house band for 'Strictly' and has recently returned to us from touring with 1D. He is ordained to perform Weddings, Christenings and Exorcisms.
