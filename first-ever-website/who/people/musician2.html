Musician

Lauren Logan

Lauren Logan is a Canadian cellist and classical violinist working as a respected session musician. Across her twelve year career, Lauren has worked with hundreds of signed bands, independent solo acts and everything in between, contributing to nineteen top 100 albums, and five feature length film soundtracks.

In this time, however, she has produced only two EPs of her own, solo work to modest acclaim and lukewarm public reception. In the run up to her first full length album, she has begun a truncated 'world' tour to promote her solo work.
