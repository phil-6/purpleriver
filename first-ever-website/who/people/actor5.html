Actor

Rodrigo Guirao Diaz

Rodrigo Guirao Diaz, serial extra and veteran of no fewer than four Spanish language soap operas, majored in theatre at the University of Texas. While he gained some small recognition amongst dedicated Latin American soap fans, Diaz has not yet attained a speaking role in any mainstream Hollywood production. A dedicated career man, Diaz sees himself as in for the long haul, maintaining a careful diction he hopes will eventually find him a role in a high-budget cable drama.

He is currently engaged in writing a theatrical retelling of El Cid Conquistador, which he will of course direct and star in.
