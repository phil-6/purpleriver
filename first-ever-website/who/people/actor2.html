Actor

Victoria Bragg

Baroness Bragg has been in the theatre for many years. Eventually the staff stopped trying to make her leave after a particularly lascivious presentation of Macbeth on Stilts and gave her a job.

She has made her career as a critic, stage-hand and all star classical Vuvuzela artist.

She currently resides in the 2nd floor broom cupboard where visitors will often hear a haunting medley of this august pensioners bodily functions echo softly through the halls
