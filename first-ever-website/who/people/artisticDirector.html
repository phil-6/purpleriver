Artistic Director

Natalya Arlovskaya

Natalya Arlovskaya was born in Kharkiv. She longed to get away and really do something with her life. She ended up attending the National University of Culture and Performing Arts in Kyiv, where she graduated from the Faculty of Choreography with the distinction of Producing and Choreographer.

She then proceeded to work at the Odessa Russian Theatre. While there she met a dashing man by the name of Edward. They fell in love and married. Sadly Edward was unable to get a visa so they had to move here.
